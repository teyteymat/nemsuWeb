Influence of Lunar Phases and Tidal Variations on Ichthyobiodiversity in Mangrove Forest in Lianga, Surigao del Sur, Philippines

Authors: Jaynos R. Cortes, Redmark A. Manatad, Mafeo Q. Lamban

This research explores how lunar phases and tidal variations affect ichthyobiodiversity in the mangrove forests of Lianga, Surigao del Sur, Philippines. It examines the relationship between natural cycles and fish diversity, providing insights into the ecological dynamics of mangrove ecosystems.

Census of Spiny Lobster Seedstock Fishery in the Settlement Areas of Lianga Bay, Philippines

Authors: Amor Rojas-Salinas and Cynthia P. Sajot

This study conducts a census of the spiny lobster seedstock fishery in the settlement areas of Lianga Bay, Philippines. It aims to assess the population size, distribution, and sustainability of spiny lobster stocks, providing crucial data for the management and conservation of this valuable marine resource in the region.

Authors: Roel C. Cabonegro, Orvin Greg J. Saga-ad, Dominador F. Bacalso, Jr., Jaynos R. Cortes

This research investigates the species richness and distribution of mud crabs (Scylla spp.) in Lianga, Surigao del Sur, Philippines. It aims to identify the variety of mud crab species present in the region and examine their spatial distribution, contributing to the understanding of their ecological roles and informing local conservation efforts for sustainable fisheries management.

Unveiling the Diversity of Pectinidae in Philippine Waters: A Taxonomic Study

Authors: Roan Gen A. Cariaga, Fabio C. Ruaza, Jr., and Jaynos R. Cortes

This research explores the diversity of Pectinidae (scallops) in Philippine waters through a taxonomic study. It aims to identify and classify different species of scallops found in the region, shedding light on their distribution, ecological significance, and potential for sustainable exploitation in local fisheries. The findings will contribute to the broader understanding of marine biodiversity in the Philippines.

This study investigates the prevalence of parasitic fauna in milkfish (*Chanos chanos*) cultured in brackishwater fishponds and marine fish pens in Surigao del Sur, Philippines.
