University Seal

The seal conveys an appropriate emblem that speaks the personality of the university, focusing on business and technical/vocational training. It symbolizes the pioneering state University in the Province of Surigao del Sur.

Ring

The seal depicts the big ring that reads: Surigao Del Sur State University or SDSSU. The ring symbolizes the continuity of leadership across SDSSU's six campuses: Tandag, Lianga, Cagwait, San Miguel, Tagbina, and Cantillan. It acts as a bridge between the University and the world, reflecting the valuable experiences gained beyond the university.

Shield

The shield represents the University's educational mission. It is quartered to represent courses offered across SDSSU campuses, including Engineering, HRM, Computer Science, Agriculture, Fisheries, Applied Sciences, Medical courses, and Trades and Industrial Arts.

Open Book

The open book symbolizes the knowledge and education provided by the University.

Torch and Laurel

The torch represents the guiding light of the SDSSU community, while the laurel symbolizes victory over global challenges faced by SDSSU graduates.
